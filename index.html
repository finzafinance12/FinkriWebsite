How it works

Three things, then it's automatic

You set it up once. After that, Finkri keeps up on its own.

1

Connect once

Allow SMS access, or upload a bank statement PDF. Finkri reads the transactions and nothing else.

2

It sorts everything

Each transaction is named and categorized automatically — Swiggy to Food, rent to Housing, a friend to Personal Transfer.

3

You see the picture

Clear weekly insights show where your money went, which categories are growing, and what to watch.

Privacy first, always

Your data never leaves your phone

Finkri keeps your transactions, balances, and account numbers on your device. Only the merchant name (the same thing visible to anyone you pay) is ever used to sort a transaction — and even that is masked.

On-device storage Amounts never uploaded No account numbers sent No ads, ever
